Refunds & cancellation

Fair, simple, and in plain English.

No fine print, no gotchas. Here's exactly how refunds and cancellation work.

01 / First-month grace

Your first payment is protected

New clients get a 3-day window from their first payment to change their mind. If you decide Echo Studio isn't right within those first 3 days and we haven't started work yet, we refund your first payment in full — no questions asked. Once we've begun your first batch, that payment is non-refundable.

02 / Ongoing billing

Renewals are non-refundable

Billing is monthly and upfront, before work begins. After your first month, each monthly payment is final. We commit production time the moment your payment clears, so we don't refund renewals.

03 / Leaving

Cancel anytime, no penalties

No long-term contracts, no termination fees. Send written notice (email is fine) to stop future billing. You'll finish out the month you've already paid for and receive all its content — then the engagement simply ends. If you cancel partway through a paid month, you keep receiving content through the end of that month.

04 / Quality issues

If you're unhappy with a batch

Every batch includes 2 rounds of revisions, so if something misses the mark, we fix it. Beyond that we handle concerns case-by-case — our default is always to make the work right rather than offer cash back.

05 / Ownership

You always keep your content

Everything we deliver is yours to keep forever, even after you cancel. The files stay in your shared folder, and you're free to use, edit, and post them however you like.

FAQ

The questions everyone asks.

Still wondering about something? Book a call and we'll answer it directly — no pressure to sign.

Will you post on my accounts for me? +
No — and that's intentional. We deliver finished content into a shared Drive folder. You post from your established accounts where you already have an audience. This protects you from low-reach issues on new accounts and keeps your relationship with your audience yours.
How long is the contract? +
Month to month. You give 30 days written notice to cancel. The first month is essentially a paid trial — if it's not working, you give notice and we wrap up cleanly. No termination fees, ever.
Can you guarantee views or follower growth? +
No one honest can. Algorithms change weekly. What we guarantee is consistent, on-brand, professional-grade content from every episode. Most clients see 3–5× more reach per episode within 60 days, but we won't promise specific numbers.
When do I pay? +
Upfront, at the start of each month, before work begins. Stripe auto-charges on the 1st. This is industry standard for retainer work and lets us commit fully to your timeline and quality.
Will the content sound like me? +
Yes — because we use your actual words. We pull text posts directly from your transcript with light editing, build carousels around your frameworks, and clip your real moments. We don't write original prose pretending to be you. This is your voice, just better packaged.
What if I want revisions? +
Every batch includes 2 rounds of revisions. Most clients use one round in the first month and zero rounds after that, once we've nailed your style guide. Additional rounds are billed hourly if needed.
Do you work with non-B2B podcasters? +
No. We specialize in B2B because the content style, audience, and ROI work differently from B2C. Staying focused makes us better at what we do. If you host a comedy, true crime, or lifestyle podcast, we're happy to refer you to studios that specialize there.
